Natura

Natura is a scenic route planner MVP that turns open map data and street-level imagery into a "most scenic" drive between two points. It pulls roads from OpenStreetMap, samples a uniform grid of points, matches those samples to Mapillary images, extracts simple scenicness cues from each image, and builds a heatmap to score OSRM route alternatives. The result is an interactive map where you can compare routes, see coverage bounds, and rerun scenic routing without re-downloading imagery.
